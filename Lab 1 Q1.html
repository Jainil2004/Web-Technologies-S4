UPES Bidholi Campus

Life at UPES Bidholi campus is a unique blend of academic rigor and a thriving campus
environment. Nestled in the scenic beauty near Dehradun, the campus offers modern
facilities, cutting-edge classrooms, and well-equipped laboratories. The vibrant
atmosphere is enhanced by a range of extracurricular activities, cultural events, and
sports facilities that contribute to a lively student community. UPES Bidholi
emphasizes industry relevance in education, organizing guest lectures and workshops
to prepare students for their professional careers. Surrounded by greenery, the
campus provides a serene backdrop for learning and personal development, creating a
memorable experience for students at UPES Bidholi.

UPES Kandoli Campus

Life at UPES Kandoli campus is an enriching experience set against the backdrop
of the scenic Himalayas. This campus, located near Dehradun, provides a unique blend of
academic excellence and a serene environment. Students at UPES Kandoli enjoy state-
of-the-art facilities, including modern classrooms, well-equipped labs, and recreational
spaces. The campus fosters a tight-knit community through various clubs, cultural
events, and sports activities. With a focus on industry-relevant education, UPES
Kandoli hosts guest lectures and workshops, ensuring students are well-prepared for
their professional journey. The lush green surroundings and a peaceful atmosphere
create an ideal setting for learning and personal growth, making life at UPES Kandoli
truly memorable.
